Welcome to SovietBot's Website

Is a Discord Bot written in Java. It uses the API Discord4J for interfacing with Discord and JDA Player. SovietBot is under heavy constant development, but it is kept online almost all the time. expect about 5-10 minutes of downtime per day for maintenance and updating.

Feel free to invite this bot to your server, it has many great features, such as

  • Music Streaming from Youtube and Soundcloud
  • Removing up to 99 messages from chat at once
  • Per user Permissions: Normal, Regular, Moderator, and Admin.
  • Funneling pure Dank into voice channels
  • Telling "In Soviet Russia" jokes

and much more...
